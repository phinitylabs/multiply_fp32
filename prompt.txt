### Critical Correctness Requirements (Must Follow)

- If exponent after normalization exceeds maximum:
  → output INF (exp = 255, mantissa = 0)
  → do NOT clamp to zero

- If exponent becomes negative after normalization:
  → output zero
  → do NOT attempt partial/denormal handling

- Normalization must happen BEFORE rounding

- Rounding must NOT be applied on unnormalized mantissa

- Perform normalization exactly once:
  → do NOT normalize again after rounding unless mantissa overflow occurs

- After rounding:
  if mantissa overflows:
    → shift right by 1
    → increment exponent

- Do NOT mix multiple scaling methods:
  → avoid using both "*4" and exponent "+1"
